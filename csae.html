Standards and Policies Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Zero Tolerance Policy

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation. Our platform is committed to protecting children and young people from harm and ensuring their safety while using our services.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action:

  • Any content involving the sexual exploitation of minors
  • Child abuse imagery or content
  • Content that sexualizes minors
  • Grooming behavior or attempts to exploit minors
  • Sharing or soliciting content related to child exploitation

Reporting and Enforcement

We take the following measures to enforce our policies:

  • Immediate removal of any content that violates these policies
  • Permanent account termination for violators
